---
title: Take notes about current chat
description: Take summarized notes and save them into the notes folder
---

Summarize the key points of this conversation and save to a markdown file.

Directory: ~/.local/share/claude-notes
Filename format: YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>.md (e.g., 2025-12-02-auth-refactor.md)
Topic hint (optional): $ARGUMENTS

Include these sections:

- **Summary**: Brief overview of what was discussed/accomplished
- **Key Decisions**: Any decisions made during the conversation
- **Code Changes**: Files modified or created (if applicable)
- **Follow-ups**: Any pending items or next steps
